Hi! I'm Sarah.

I'm a senior undergraduate student studying Computer Information Systems (CIS) at ASU.

When I first entered ASU, I was studying Biochemistry with the goal of going to medical school. Although I had a great interest in healthcare, I realized that I didn't want to practice it. I changed majors from Biochemistry to Marketing on a whim after my first semester. For a while, I still felt lost and didn't know what career to pursue. After my sophomore year, my friend convinced me to switch my major over to CIS. I agreed because I had taken an introductory course to computer applications and found it interesting. Ever since then, I've enjoyed pretty much everything I've learned about the field. Right now, I'm learning to apply Python, HTML, and CSS in my coursework and am eager to learn more.

Outside of academics, I enjoy nature, art, and jazz. I also love traveling. Most recently, I went to the city of Avalon in Santa Catalina Island. You can check out some pictures I took there below!
